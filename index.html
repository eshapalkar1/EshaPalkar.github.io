Nice, France

Nice is a charming city located on the French Riviera, known for its beautiful beaches, crystal clear waters, and stunning landscapes. Its colorful old town, also known as Vieux Nice, is filled with narrow streets, quaint shops, and a vibrant local culture. The city also boasts a wide variety of museums, art galleries, and historic landmarks such as the famous Promenade des Anglais and the Castle Hill with its panoramic views of the city.

Eze is a picturesque village located on a hilltop overlooking the Mediterranean Sea on the French Riviera. Its charming narrow streets, ancient stone buildings, and breathtaking panoramic views of the coastline make it a popular tourist destination. Eze is home to many historic landmarks such as the Eze Castle ruins and the Chapelle de la Sainte Croix. The village is also famous for its exotic garden, which showcases a wide range of succulent plants and cacti.
